lisa søndergaard

aspiring frontnd.ninja at your service

[ p e r s o n ]

My name is Lisa and if you’re reading this paragraph, then you found my portfolio. I'm a student in multimedia design at KEA, an education that focuses on enhancing digital skills in many areas.

During my 2 ½ semester I’ve come into contact with video production, sound editing, designing digital graphics, drawing illustrations, website production, UI design, game creation, animations and programming. And that’s just on the creative side. SEO, UX, iterative testing methods and agile work processes like SCRUM and Design Sprint has also been on the curriculum.

For the 3rd semester we were to pick an elective to create our professional profile. For me there were no doubts: I wanted to primarily program and secondly design user interfaces, so I chose the profile frontend design. To me there is nothing more rewarding than to see a design being built from skratch through code.

In my professional life I am meticulous, thorough, and a bit of a perfectionist, but also encouraging, helpful and resourceful when it comes to being a teamplayer. This is something that comes in handy in my student job as a tutor in multimedia design.
Structure is important to me -you know the person who's always got several handwritten lists on their desk, so they can keep track of the tasks even if there's already a scrum board for the team? That's me. And I find it a little more satisfying than I should, when I can cross off an item!

On the social side, I find it very important to connect with my co-workers and to socialize without it necessarily being in relation to the job. Maybe while drinking a beer or two when a project is done.

My ambition is to apply for the bahelor programme in web development at KEA after my last semester of multimedia design. This is actually why I started at KEA in the first place, and I can’t wait to get an even more detailed look into the world of coding languages and web development.
